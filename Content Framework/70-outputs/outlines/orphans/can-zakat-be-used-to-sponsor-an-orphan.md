## FAQ

### Can zakat be used to sponsor an orphan?

Yes, when the child or household is zakat-eligible and the funds are handled through a proper zakat process.

### Does every orphan qualify for zakat?

No. Orphan status alone is not always enough. The child or household must qualify under zakat recipient categories, commonly poverty or need.

### What if I am not sure the orphan sponsorship is zakat-eligible?

Ask the charity how zakat funds are handled. If eligibility is not confirmed, sadaqah or general orphan support may be a safer giving route.

### Is sadaqah different from zakat?

Yes. Zakat is an obligation with recipient rules. Sadaqah is voluntary charity and is generally broader.

### Can I support orphans through LifeUSA?

Yes. LifeUSA has orphan support and sponsorship-related programs. If you intend the gift as zakat, confirm the current zakat-eligible designation before donating.
