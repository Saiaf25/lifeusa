Short answer

Zakat can support orphaned children when the child or household qualifies under zakat eligibility rules. Orphan status alone is not always enough.

Research note

External sources treat this as a real donor question. Use the linked sources to explain eligibility carefully without borrowing another charity's policy claims.

Article distinction

Safe fallback
